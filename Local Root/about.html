About Us

30 years of solid experience

Our family enterprise was established in 1980 and it will be our pleasure to personally take care of you and assist with your individual travel arrangements - specially tailored for Western Canada and the USA - so you may experience a Holiday that exceeds your expectations.

We are located in Langdale/Gibsons on the Sunshine Coast (see also map here), a short and beautiful ferry ride from Horseshoe Bay, which is 30 min past West Vancouver. It is ideally located for touring the B.C. mainland and Vancouver Island, in your motorhome.

Transfers to and from our property are included in our prices. (Does not apply to short term rentals under 10 days). We will arrange for a taxi to meet you at the Airport or at a Vancouver hotel, and bring you to the ferry terminal in Horseshoe Bay (just past West Vancouver). After a short ferry ride, which is already the beginning of a great holiday and a great ride through paradise, we will meet you and bring you to your motorhome. After you have returned the vehicle to us, we will arrange for a taxi to take you back to Vancouver.

Our motorhomes are all kept in very good interior, exterior and mechanical condition. We take a real pride in the way we look after you during your vacation. Your transfer from the airport or your hotel will be arranged with your booking. When you arrive at our property, your motorhome will be waiting for you and will be travel-ready.

This means beds made, kitchen equipped and cupboards clean for your personal items. Your motorhome will have full gasoline and propane tanks. You will also find many extras to make your trip more enjoyable (at no extra cost).
