Tour

Here are suggestions of Circle Tours though BC

  • Circle Tour of the Sunshine Coast Great tour for 7 - 10 days

    Drive up the Sunshine Coast to Powell River, cross over to Vancouver Island and come back via Nanaimo or Victoria. Don’t miss whale watching! Up and down the coast of BC you can find Orcas (killer whales), Gray whales and Humpback whales.

  • Circle Tour to the Rockies and Banff National Park. Great tour for 14 - 21 days.

    From Horseshoe Bay drive North to Whistler, from Clearwater to Jasper National Park, down to Banff National Park and coming back via TransCanada No.1 through Golden,Kamloops, Cache Creek and via the Fraser Valley back to Vancouver. On your way through the Fraser Valley visit Eagle Wing RV Park in Aldergrove...

  • Cruise on a historic wooden vessel in spectacular wildlife surroundings

    Before or after your motorhome tour through BC, visit scenic Gibsons Landing on the Sunshine Coast where the former Tug Boat "Reliant" is moored and spend time visiting the town made famous by the TV show Beachcombers. There are great restaurants, art galleries, and a scenic sea wall you can stroll on right beside the dock. It is perfect!!

  • After a relaxing day in Gibsons Landing, depart aboard Reliant at sunset for your special destinations.

    There is so much to see. Whales, eagles and bears abound. Hike in a rainforest, kayak on glass calm waters, fish for big salmon and halibut then spend the evening relaxing around the barbeque on Reliant's spacious stern deck.

  • Reliant Eco Tours will provide you with the most wonderful and economical marine eco tours anywhere on the coastal waters of British Columbia.
